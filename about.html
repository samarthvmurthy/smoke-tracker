About Us
Smoke Free — Track your resistance
Our Mission

Smoke Free is a minimalist tool built for people who want to quit smoking — or at least understand their habits better. We believe accountability and community are the two most powerful forces in breaking a dependency.

Track how long you resist the urge. Compete (or collaborate) with friends. Celebrate milestones. No ads, no paywalls, no noise.

What It Does
  • Timer that counts every second since your last cigarette
  • Session log that records each resistance streak you complete
  • Personal best tracking so you always have a goal to beat
  • Friends system — send requests, accept connections, cheer each other on
  • Live leaderboard ranked by current resistance time
  • Profile with custom username and avatar
  • Fully responsive — works on phone, tablet, and desktop
