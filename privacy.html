Privacy Policy
Effective date: March 12, 2026
The Short Version
We collect only what is needed to run your account and the social features. We do not sell your data. We do not show ads. We do not share your information with third parties except Supabase, which stores it on our behalf.
Who We Are

Smoke Free ("we", "us", "our") is a web and Android app that helps you track cigarette-resistance streaks and stay accountable with friends. The app is operated by an individual developer and is available at smokefreetimer.com.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at support@smokefreetimer.com.

Data We Collect

We collect only the data required to provide the service.

Data Why Required?
Email address Account creation, sign-in, and password recovery Yes
Username Displayed on your profile, leaderboard, and friends list Yes
Session data
(start time, end time)		 Powers your resistance timer, personal best, and history log Yes
Friend connections Enables the friends list and leaderboard features No — optional feature

We do not collect your real name, phone number, location, device identifiers, or any sensitive personal information.

How We Store Your Data

All data is stored in Supabase (supabase.com), a managed cloud database and authentication platform. Supabase acts as a data processor on our behalf and stores data in secure, hosted PostgreSQL databases. Supabase's privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.

Data is transmitted over HTTPS at all times. We do not store passwords in plain text — authentication is handled entirely by Supabase Auth.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate the app:

— Authenticate you when you sign in
— Display your resistance timer and session history
— Show your username and stats on the leaderboard
— Match friend requests by username
— Send a password-recovery email if requested

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, machine learning, or any purpose other than running the features described above.

Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for their own purposes. The only external service that processes your data is Supabase, which stores it on our behalf as described above.

The app loads fonts from Google Fonts and icons from Font Awesome CDN. These are static asset requests; no account data is transmitted to them.

Data Retention & Account Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account exists. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, contact us at support@smokefreetimer.com and we will permanently delete your account, sessions, and friend connections within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Smoke Free is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@smokefreetimer.com.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
